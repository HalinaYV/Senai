About Khajiit's:

"Khajiit are commonly considered one of the beast folks, one of the few survivors of the original inhabitants of Tamriel before the coming of mer and man, and Elsweyr is their home." ―Imperial Geographic Society[src]
  • Their body shape is influenced by the moon phase

    		•
  • In Morrowind they are not seen using gloves or boots

    		•
  • In Skyrim they kept the moon sugar and Skooma market

    		•
  • In TES: Online they are seen as common housecats as well
